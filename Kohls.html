During Summer 2012 I received an amazing opportunity to intern for Kohl’s in Web Merchandise Presentation.
During my time at Kohl’s I was given responsibility for ad sets, assorting, attributing, and many other areas.
Not only did I get a well-rounded experience because of taking on these roles, but these responsibilities also
prepared me for the time at the end of the summer when my coordinator went on vacation and I was assigned
these aspects of her job. In addition to the day-to-day practices that I learned, I was also part of a team with
four other interns. Over eight weeks we worked closely together to create an analysis of the Millennial generation
and recommendations for Kohl’s. We gave a presentation of our findings to sixty people including upper management.
The ideas were so well received that parts will be implemented and we were asked to repeat the presentation for
the buying team. This was an amazing learning experience in team work, organization, and stress management.
