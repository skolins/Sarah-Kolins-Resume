During the summer of 2011 I had the opportunity to work in the Marketing Department of Beaumont Health System.
Beaumont is a three-hospital health system in metropolitan Detroit and is a leader in health education and patient care.
During my time at Beaumont I was given a significant amount of responsibility in both the web and media marketing
departments. One of my best experiences of the summer was participating in meetings with the different clinical
departments to discuss their marketing needs. Based on the information gathered in the meetings, I created marketing
content such as a video script and a radio advertisement. Please see below for examples of work on the Beaumont
Women's Health website and Facebook page.
